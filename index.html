About Our School

Shree Shanti Namuna Secondary School is a community-based educational institution located in Manigram, Tilottama Municipality, Rupandehi District, Nepal. Established with the support of local educators and the community, the school provides quality education from Montessori to higher secondary (+2) level in both Nepali and English mediums. It is known for its focus on academic excellence, moral values, discipline, and overall personality development of students, along with active participation in sports and co-curricular activities

Vision & Mission

Vision:
To nurture responsible, skilled, and compassionate citizens who contribute positively to society, uphold ethical values, and excel academically and socially in a rapidly changing world.

Mission:
Provide quality education through innovative teaching methods and modern technology. Foster moral values, discipline, and creativity among students. Encourage participation in sports, arts, and co-curricular activities for holistic development. Promote critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and lifelong learning. Build an inclusive and supportive learning environment for all students.
